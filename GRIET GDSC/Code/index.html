Developer Student Clubs GRIET

The GRIET Google Developer Student Club is like a tech playground! Our student leaders guide this energetic community where we all come together to learn, team up, and create cool projects using Google tech. It's not just a club; it's where our passion for technology meets hands-on fun!

What does GDSC-GRIET do?

At GDSC-GRIET, our team of tech enthusiasts collaborates on exciting projects to create real-life solutions.
We develop products that make a meaningful impact, all while providing a platform for students to expand their knowledge in various tech domains. Join us as we blend passion with practicality, turning ideas into valuable contributions to the tech world.
